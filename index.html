Welcome To Our College

Maharaja Purna Chandra Bhanja Deo and ,Christened after the Late Maharaja Purna Chandra Bhanja Deo, the enlightened ruler of the erstwhile Princely state of Mayurbhanj, the College has been successfully catering to the need of higher education of the people, not only of Orissa but also of its adjoining states since its inception in July 1948.
